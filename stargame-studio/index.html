Our story

About Stargame Studio

"Stargame Studio is your youth-powered creative force, turning ROBLOX community ideas into stunning realities. From immersive experiences to UGC, trendy 2D fashion to captivating artwork — if you can imagine it on ROBLOX, Steam, or beyond, we're here to make it happen. Driven by passionate young developers, we thrive on friendship, open communication, and unstoppable teamwork."

Artists first — strict no-AI art policy

Stargame Studio unequivocally values original artistic creation. AI-generated art is not a substitute for human artistry. We champion traditional and digital art forms, actively recruiting human artists. AI may only be used for reference/inspiration — never submitted as original work.

Developer structure & compensation

Tiered system: Full-Time (revenue share on milestones), Part-Time (proportional to tenure), Commission-Based (paid per project). Payments via USD/PayPal, Robux, revenue share, gift cards, Discord Nitro. All contributors credited.

Youth-powered — especially ages 13–19

We particularly champion young developers and artists aged 13–19, providing a platform to build real skills, real portfolios, and real experience — proving age is no barrier to professional quality output.

Safety, conduct & community

Full Code of Conduct in force as of March 2025. Respect, inclusivity, cyber safety, and professional communication are non-negotiable. Appeals Department available. All studio communications are family-friendly.
